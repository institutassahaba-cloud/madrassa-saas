# Product

## Register

product

## Users

MadrassaApp is used by the institute director, secretary, and teachers. Teachers work mainly from their dashboard to find their students, check lesson notebooks, and follow scheduled lessons. The director and secretary use the app to supervise students, groups, payments, planning, and operational follow-up.

## Product Purpose

The product replaces a frozen Google Sheets workflow with a multi-tenant SaaS for Institut As-Sahaba. Success means reliable access to migrated student, group, teacher, lesson, payment, notebook, and schedule data, with fewer manual lookups and safer role-specific workflows.

## Brand Personality

Calm, trustworthy, and respectful. The interface should feel practical and serene, supporting daily administrative and teaching work without decorative noise.

## Anti-references

Avoid marketing-site theatrics, oversized hero layouts, playful dashboards, dense decoration, and visual patterns that make routine work feel less serious. Avoid hiding important school operations behind vague labels or unfamiliar controls.

## Design Principles

- Put the working information close to the user's current task.
- Prefer clear, compact dashboard patterns over expressive visual novelty.
- Keep role boundaries obvious and safe, especially for teacher view and director impersonation.
- Use calm hierarchy: important details should be easy to scan, not loud.
- Preserve migrated data faithfully and make uncertainty visible.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Aim for readable contrast, keyboard-accessible controls, clear focus states, and labels that work for French-speaking users. Avoid relying on color alone for status or meaning.
